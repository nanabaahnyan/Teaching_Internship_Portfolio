Baidoo Bonsoe Senior High Technical School Elective ICT SHS 1 1st Semester, 2025 Ezekiel Baah

Overview & Objectives

This scheme of work outlines the planned teaching and learning activities for the Elective ICT subject for SHS 1 first semester. It covers the scope and sequence of topics to be taught, the methods to be used, resources required, and the assessment approach for each week.

  • Align lessons with the GES ICT curriculum for Senior High School.
  • Ensure balanced coverage of theory and practical components.
  • Integrate diverse TLMs to cater for different learning styles.
  • Embed continuous formative assessment throughout.
