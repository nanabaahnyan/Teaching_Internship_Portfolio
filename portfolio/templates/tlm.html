About My TLMs

Teaching and Learning Materials (TLMs) are an essential part of effective instruction. During my internship, I designed and used a variety of materials to make abstract ICT concepts concrete, engaging, and accessible for all learners. Below is a showcase of the key TLMs I developed, accessed, and used.

Reflection on TLM Usage

The process of designing and utilizing these TLMs was a pivotal part of my teaching internship, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical classroom application. I found that a blended approach—combining digital, visual, and tactile materials—was the most effective strategy for catering to the diverse learning styles of my students.
