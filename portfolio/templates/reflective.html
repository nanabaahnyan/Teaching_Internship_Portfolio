"We do not learn from experience — we learn from reflecting on experience."
— John Dewey  |  The cornerstone of my reflective practice

Reflective Framework Used

My reflective practice is guided by Gibbs' Reflective Cycle (1988), which provides a structured framework for thinking critically about teaching experiences. Each reflection follows the six stages below:

1

Description

What happened?

2

Feelings

What did you think & feel?

3

Evaluation

What was good / bad?

4

Analysis

Why did it happen?

5

Conclusion

What else could you have done?

6

Action Plan

What will you do next time?
